About Us

We are a family-owned choose-and-cut Christmas tree farm located in Hammonton, New Jersey. We open the day after Thanksgiving and offer a variety of evergreen trees, wreaths, poinsettias, and more. Enjoy hayrides, hot chocolate, and cookies while selecting your perfect tree. We accept cash, Venmo, and PayPal.

Our farm has been providing holiday joy for years, with fresh trees planted annually to ensure sustainability.
